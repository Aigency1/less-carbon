What moves your price up the curve

The intake form asks structured questions about each of these inputs. Pricing is calculated automatically once you submit. No surprises after.

Operational sites

Single site is the floor. Each additional site adds setup, boundary and data-handling complexity.

Fleet and equipment

Number and class of vehicles (passenger, light commercial, rigid, articulated, off-road, marine) and any mobile plant.

Stationary fuels

Natural gas, LPG, diesel generators, boilers, process heat. Each fuel type adds a measurement boundary and source-document check.

Refrigeration and HVAC

Cold rooms, commercial HVAC, refrigerated transport. Refrigerant tracking is a separate calculation pathway.

Employees and turnover

Used to size organisational scope. Headcount and turnover band shift the assessment band, not pad the bill.

ANZSIC industry and process

Industry classification surfaces sector-specific emissions sources (process, fugitive, agricultural) that change the methodology.
