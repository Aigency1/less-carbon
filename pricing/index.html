Pricing questions

Common questions.

How much does a Less Carbon assessment cost?

Assessments start from $995 + GST. That's the base price; your exact fixed fee is calculated automatically from your operations, sites, fleet, energy use and industry during the intake process, and you see it in full before you pay.

Why isn't there one flat price?

Because a sole trader running one office has a fraction of the emissions complexity of a business with three sites, a delivery fleet and commercial refrigeration. A flat price means overcharging simple businesses or underpricing complex ones. You pay for exactly what your assessment requires, nothing more.

Is the price locked once it's calculated?

Yes. The fee shown in the intake form is the fee you pay. No adjustment invoices after you've submitted your records, and no extras for the certificate or the register listing.

What exactly is included in the fee?

The full Scope 1 and 2 assessment, professional review, your Supplier Emissions Certificate, your SME Emissions Assessment Report, and 12 months on the Less Carbon Certificate Register: everything you need to respond to a Scope 3 supplier request.

Ready to see your price?

The intake form calculates your exact fee, from $995 + GST, and gets your assessment started. Around 10 minutes, and you see the price before you pay.
