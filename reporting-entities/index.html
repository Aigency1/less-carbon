Program questions, answered straight

What reporting entities ask us.

If yours isn't here, the methodology page goes deeper.

What does a bulk supplier program include?

Managed supplier outreach, intake and data collection, a Carbon Assessment and Supplier Emissions Certificate for every supplier, automated follow-up on missing information, and structured progress reporting back to you.

What do we receive at the end of a batch?

A structured delivery pack: completed supplier certificates, a supplier emissions summary, batch completion report, supplier status report, unresolved supplier list, data quality notes and recommended next steps.

How standardised is the supplier data?

Every supplier is assessed on the same methodology and reported in the same format. No interpreting, massaging or re-keying thousands of different spreadsheets into your systems.

How fast can a program start?

A pilot wave can be live within 30 days of scoping. Full programs are scheduled to your reporting calendar and rolled out in batches.

How is a program priced?

Programs are priced per supplier, based on each supplier's completed intake, business profile and assessment requirements. For eligible programs of 50+ suppliers, the Reporting Entity receives a 20% bulk-program discount on the final price for each participating supplier. Your scoping proposal sets out the recommended batch size, process, timeline and discount structure. The final program cost is confirmed progressively as suppliers complete their intake.

Do our suppliers pay anything?

No. Suppliers in your program are covered by the program. They complete a short online intake, upload their records and receive their own Carbon Assessment and Supplier Emissions Certificate.
