Methodology Basis
Methodology Basis

Clear basis, without exposing the working engine

This assessment has been prepared using client-submitted activity data and supporting records. Greenhouse gas emissions are assessed on a Scope 1 and Scope 2 basis with reference to the GHG Protocol Corporate Standard and applicable Australian emissions factors and guidance relevant to AASB S2-related reporting.

Included in the final report

  • Reporting period, entity and operational boundary.
  • Source category emissions summary.
  • Data coverage, assumptions and limitations.
  • Report reference and related certificate details.

Not shown in this public sample

  • Detailed calculation records and factor-by-factor workings.
  • Client documents, invoices and source files.
  • Site-by-site activity schedules.
  • Commercial or confidential supplier information.

Important report limitation

This document does not constitute financial audit, statutory assurance or independent verification unless expressly stated otherwise. Results are based on the information and supporting records made available by the client for the reporting period.

Where records are incomplete, unavailable or only partly available, the final report notes the relevant assumption, exclusion or limitation.
